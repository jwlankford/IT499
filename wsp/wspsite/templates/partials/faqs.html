Frequently Asked Questions

“Sip, Savor, and Watermelon Wonders: Your Watermelon Seltzer Questions Answered!” 🍉🥂

Is watermelon hard seltzer made from real watermelons?

While we’d love to say our seltzers are freshly squeezed from watermelons, the truth is they’re crafted with love and a dash of imagination. 🍉

Can I use watermelon hard seltzer as sunscreen?

As much as we’d like to claim SPF 50 protection, our seltzers are best enjoyed in a chilled glass, not slathered on your skin. 😎

Does watermelon seltzer make you dance better?

We can’t guarantee dance moves, but we’ve seen some impressive watermelon-inspired twirls after a can or two. 💃

How many watermelon seltzers can I drink before turning into an actual watermelon?

We recommend moderation, but rest assured, transformation into a fruit is unlikely. 🤔

Can I mix watermelon seltzer with tequila for a wild party?

While we don’t endorse wild parties (wink), a splash of tequila might just turn your watermelon seltzer into a fiesta. 🎉
