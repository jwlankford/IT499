Welcome to “XThirst”

At XThirst, we believe that every sip should be an escape to sun-kissed beaches, even if you’re just lounging on your couch. Our watermelon-infused hard seltzers are the ultimate ode to summer, blending the juiciness of real watermelons with the effervescence of pure refreshment. Dive in and discover the taste of endless summer in every can.

Why XThirst?

Crafted with Love: Our master brewers handpick the ripest watermelons, capturing their essence in every batch. Each can is a labor of love, infused with the spirit of sun-soaked afternoons.

Authentic Flavor: No shortcuts here! We’ve perfected the delicate balance of sweet and refreshing watermelon flavor. Close your eyes, take a sip, and let the waves of nostalgia wash over you.

Zero Guilt, All Pleasure: With only 100 calories per can and zero added sugar, XThirst lets you indulge guilt-free. It’s like a guilt-free beach vacation—minus the sand in your shoes.
